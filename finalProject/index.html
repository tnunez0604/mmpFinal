The Punishment for Police Brutality is… Suspension and Resignation?

by Tamara Nuñez

In many cases, the cops have abused their power and harmed the lives of many people they have pursued, as well as their families. For these crimes (some being fatal), they have faced light penalties.

Eric Garner Case

Eric Garner was restrained by a chokehold, and died from lack of oxygen, pleading for the officer to let him go.

Villanueva case

"After speeding down a road at nearly 90 mph, Pedro Villanueva hit a dead end, turned his truck around and barreled toward the car that had been chasing him. Two plainclothes officers who had followed Villanueva in an unmarked car opened fire, leaving the 19-year-old man dead and a passenger injured..."
